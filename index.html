About Me

I am a senior year undergrad student in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at University of Rajshahi, Bangladesh. I am interested in exploring Robot Collaboration in the context of industrial robots. My area of interest are Robotics, Computer Vision, Control Theory, Machine Learning, Embedded System Designing. My goal is to establish an independent research career with multidisciplinary collaborations to approach problems related to Robotics and Artificially Intelligent Systems.


From my junior year I am working with Johirul Islam Tusher, Lecturer Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology on the supervision of Shaikh Khaled Mostaque, Assistant Professor, University of Rajshahi on a Computer Vision research project.


I have been involved in projects in the domain of Robotics, Control of Mobile Robots, Robotics Simulation with ROS/Webots, Machine Learning and Deep Learning for Computer Vision. Additionally I am interested in the area of Self Driving Cars and UAVs.
